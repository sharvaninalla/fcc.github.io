About us

Welcome to our studio in Hyderabad - a place for yoga, a space for the soul.It's all about you. We believe that yoga is for everyone and we focus on your path to awaken,nurture and know your true essence.

Our aim is to be inclusive and share tools with you to become healthier, happier, and live a more self-aware concious life. We strive to cultivate a community that is supportive without judgement where everyone fits in and can realize their full potential.

Our classes

Hatha yoga

Hatha yoga is the branch of yoga that typically comes to mind when you think of yoga in general terms. With Hatha yoga there is no need for you to feel like your life is out of control. You learn to direct and master your energy.

This practice involves breath, body and mind and classes are usually 45 minutes to 90 minutes of breathing, yoga poses and meditation.
